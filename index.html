Biography

I am a research fellow at Microsoft Research India, where I work with Amit Deshpande and Navin Goyal. My current research focuses on understanding deep generative models through theory and experiments.

Previously, I obtained my B.Tech. in Computer Science from International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad (IIIT-H), where I worked with Naresh Manwani from Machine Learning Lab at IIIT-H.

I like to (a) understand machine learning algorithms using theory and experiments and (b) propose new algorithms which have theoretical guarantees. Currently, I am exploring topics in representation learning, unsupervised learning, optimization. I am also interested in applications of machine learning in computer vision and natural language processing.
