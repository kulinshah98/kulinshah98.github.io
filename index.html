About me

I am a second-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science department at the University of Texas Austin advised by Prof. Qiang Liu and Prof. Adam Klivans. Previously, I was a research fellow at Microsoft Research India, where I worked with Amit Deshpande and Navin Goyal. I graduated with my B.Tech. (Hons.) in Computer Science from International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad (IIIT-H), where I worked with Naresh Manwani.

I like to work on the theoretical and empirical foundations of machine learning. I seek to understand and gain insights into practical machine learning algorithms using theory and experiments, and using those insights, propose new algorithms with theoretical guarantees to benefit practice.
